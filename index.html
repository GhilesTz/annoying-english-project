About the School

Founded in 2010, the National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) trains engineers and researchers ready to tackle contemporary technological challenges. Our mission is to combine academic excellence, innovation, and professional integration to prepare our students for international careers.

ENSTA offers accredited programs in computer engineering, telecommunications, mechanics, and energy, supported by cutting-edge research laboratories. We maintain national and international industrial and academic partnerships to provide internships, real-world projects, and exchange opportunities.
